About Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Located in southwestern Uganda, Bwindi is one of the most biodiverse areas in the world. It is home to over half of the world’s remaining mountain gorillas, making it a must-visit destination for wildlife enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Activities in Bwindi

  • Gorilla Trekking: Embark on a guided trek to observe mountain gorillas in their natural habitat.
  • Birdwatching: Spot over 350 bird species, including 23 endemic to the Albertine Rift.
  • Nature Walks: Explore the park’s trails and enjoy its lush vegetation and scenic views.
  • Cultural Tours: Visit local communities to learn about their traditions and way of life.
